Who am I?

My name is Juan Garzon, I am a Bimorphic Artist. I've adopted Joan Miro's style of Biomorphism and recreate it into my own style.
Each drawing, painting, mural, or print is created with emotion, intention, consideration to my viewers.
I want the world to see my art and have a unique visual experience.
